Did you know that octopuses have three hearts? Two of these hearts pump blood to the gills to get oxygen, while the third heart circulates the oxygenated blood to the rest of the body. Interestingly, when an octopus swims, the heart that delivers blood to the body stops beating, which is why these creatures prefer to crawl rather than swim, as swimming can exhaust them. Octopuses are truly remarkable and fascinating creatures of the ocean!
